Pune

Pune was the capital of the Maratha Empire, and hence has a huge cultural and historical tale to tell of its glorious past. From then to the present date, Pune city has undergone a 360-degree transformation. Today, Pune is known to be an educational hub and after Bangalore, an important center for IT services too.

While there is no dearth of good eateries in Pune, street food is what is really famous here. Since it is a University town, food is affordable and available in a whole lot of variety. From Pav Bhajis, Vada Pavs, Momos to mouthwatering sweet dishes, it caters to everyone’s tastes. So, if you are not high on formal dining, Pune is the best place to be for street food lovers.

Shopping in Pune is a lot of fun too, as there is a huge variety of stuff at a reasonable rate, mainly catering to the student communities. You can really get excellent deals on goods at the Fergusson College Road, ? Hong Kong Lane, MG Road and Fashion Street.

Shaniwarwada

The city of Pune, appeals to many due to its various tourist attractions that range from gardens, museums to architectural monuments. Apart from vacations, business is one of the main reasons people visit Pune. There are a number of places of architectural importance like Sinhagad and Khadakwasala, Aga Khan Palace, Shaniwarwada and so on. There are also other places of interests like, the Osho Ashram.
