Know more about Flat Fare zones

Ola recognizes some key zones in your city. At these locations, you can avail flat pricing for Ola share upto fixed kilometers and during defined hours. Flat Fare zones provide more control & predictability to how much you need to spend.

Follow the links to know the flat-fares for popular cities around India.
