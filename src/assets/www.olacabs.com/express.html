Know more about Express

Express is the new pocket-friendly way to travel within the city during peak hours. Get on board an Ola Share that’s on the fastest route with no deviations. Walk to your pickup point on the Express route to board the cab and get down within a walking distance from your destination.

When you choose to book an Express ride, your pickup and drop point will be indicated on the Ola app. Your pickup and drop point are the designated Ola pickup/drop points and cannot be changed anytime during the trip. You will need to walk to the pickup point to board the Express ride.

Similarly, you will be dropped at the nearest drop point to your destination as indicated on the Ola App before you confirm the booking.

We request you to update your Ola app by visiting your phone's app store.

Please note, Express is available only on specific routes in the city and during certain hours of the day (depending on the demand on those routes).

You will be prompted to book an Express ride (depending on the availability at the time of booking) when you request a Share ride on your Ola app. On selecting the Express option, your pickup and drop point will be indicated on the Ola App.

You will need to walk to the designated pickup point to board your Express ride. You will dropped at the nearest drop point to your destination as indicated on the Ola App before you confirm the booking.

Share Pass will be auto applied to all your Express rides. However, Share pass doesn't get applied to those Share rides where total fare is cheaper than the pass fare.
